Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 19, 2026

Mayone Co., Ltd. ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use the DuoCam mobile application.

1. Camera and Microphone Data

DuoCam is designed to process dual-camera recording instantly. All video and photo processing occurs directly on your device. We do not transmit, upload, or store your recorded media on any external servers. Your media is saved exclusively to your local Apple Photos Library.

2. Information We Collect

Since the application functions primarily offline, we collect minimal data:

  • Device Information: Aggregated, anonymous analytics regarding device types and crash reports to improve application stability.
  • Purchase Data: Controlled entirely through Apple App Store services. We do not see your credit card or personalized billing data.

3. Third-Party Analytics

We may use anonymous third-party analytical tools to track application usage telemetry. These tools do not associate data with your personal identity or access your privately recorded media.

4. True Depth API & Background Segmentation

In certain recording modes involving real-time background removal, DuoCam may utilize Apple's TrueDepth or segmentation APIs. This facial/body mapping data is processed in real time and is immediately discarded. It is not saved, stored, or shared.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by publishing the updated text within the app or on this webpage.

6. Contact Us

If you have questions regarding your privacy, please email us at mayoneltd2026@gmail.com.
